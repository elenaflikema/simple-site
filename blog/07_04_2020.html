I have been seeing a lot of complaints on social media recently. People have been objecting to the "stay home" orders initiated by their governments and decide that they know what is best. The evidence is telling. Videos and photos of hundreds of people laying on beaches, spending time in public places, parents allowing their kids to mingle with others on the playground, etc. A lack of accountability, selfishness, and carelessness are some reasons that this virus is going to be dragged out. Many more people are going to suffer due to others ignorance and self-centeredness. It's a pity.

I believe that a great deal of people are living in denial about the changes we are going through, the adaptations which we need to make in order to survive this period. Why are people refusing to accept the reality? A few reasons

Fear

Inherently, humans fear what they can't relate to, don't understand, and don't have the answers to. We don't like the unknown, we prefer to live in our safe, sheltered version of reality rather than accept the truth. We can't see what the future holds,we want to know what tomorrow brings. We haven't found a light at the end of the tunnel yet. We have a million questions, but few answers. A lack of clarity. Governments tell us something one day, the next they backtrack on what they have said and say the opposite. Confusion............stress........

Resistance to adaptation and evolution

We are being forced to be like water, adjusting to the situation daily. Some people are absolutely uncompromising in their views and ideas and unwilling to be flexible. Many people have lost their jobs or have been placed on "forced vacation" lately. Yes, it is an uncomfortable situation. It isn't unique to just you or I. Instead of panicking and thinking about the fact that you don't have a job, look at the bigger picture. Millions of people just like you and I are going through the same thing right now. Perspective is key. Adapt and overcome.

The herd mentality

Media, social media, talking heads, celebrities. There is so much garbage spreading around. One person hears something which is a convenient point of view and they jump on that ship without checking the hull for a gaping breach or a qualified captain. This information gets passed around, more sailors are recruited to man the ship. Result-the herd aggressively validates this idea, conspiracy theory, point of view and goes on a campaign, promoting it to the masses.

What can we do to alleviate our stresses, reduce our fears, and transform these negatives into positives? Most of us have been afforded much more free time recently. Instead of sulking in our homes, stressing about the fact that we have to stay home, and allowing negativity to bring us down, we can all do things to distract us from the current situation.

We all have more time to spend with our family and loved ones(assuming that you share the same house). Full-time jobs and usual routines take us away from those most important to us. Embrace this time with them!

The internet is the world's biggest database of information. People always find excuses to put off doing something.

"I want to go back to school, but I'm too busy with work".

"I want to ______, but it doesn't fit my schedule".

I'll bet you can't say that now. Online education has been developing over the past decade and is seeing a surge in demand and popularity. Work on that university degree, take a self-development course, study a foreign language(like English). There are hundreds of options out there and some are even free of charge! Contact me when you have questions about studying English by way of Zoom or Skype with me!

Destress! Unwind! Catch up on a TV series that has been on your "to do" list for awhile, watch some movies, read a book or two, experiment with new recipes in the kitchen. Oh, and if you are a child at heart and enjoy playing video games, now is the time to get through one of those epics games such as The Witcher, Dragon Age, and the Final Fantasy 7 remake which releases in three days! Take advantage of this time to relax at home. Once things go back to normal, people will eventually go back to complaining about not having enough time to relax. Get it in now!

"Get the best out of life, don't let life best you"

J. S. Flickema

Pay attention to the highlighted constructions

have/has+3rd form

Present Perfect Simple

-it has many functions

If you scroll up and take a look, you will see these constructions used with recently and lately a few times. One function of present perfect is to say that something happened not long ago in the past.

Key indicators: recently, lately, just

-using the word "ago" does not fit with present perfect

I ate lunch five minutes ago.

I have just eaten lunch

Both statements express the fact that the action happened not long ago, however "ago" is a reference of finished time and cannot be used with present perfect

There are a few more functions of this tense. For more information, get ahold of me and lets take some steps to improving your English skills!
